An author by heart and a neurosurgeon by choice.

My ambition in life.

Being an author and a nuerosurgeon, at the same time, is a constant in my life.

Ever since I was a child, I have always wanted to be a doctor. I didn't have a specialty that I was interested in then, but I was really determined to be a doctor. Together with the blossoming dream of becoming an author, I discovered the specialization that I wanted once I become a medical practitioner. At first it was to be a General Surgeon, then I just found myself falling in love more with the neurosurgery department.
